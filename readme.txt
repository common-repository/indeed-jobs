Let us help you hire - It’s completely free and easy to set-up

[Indeed](http://www.indeed.com/ "Indeed | one search. all jobs.") is the world's #1 job site and enables you to create an automatically synced job section via our Jobs plugin.

Make more quality hires faster by connecting with possible candidates on desktop and mobile and target them with your own Jobs section in your WordPress page.

**What this plugin offers you**

* Follows your website template
    - No additional effort necessary to match your branding.
* Always up to date list of all your live Indeed jobs
    - If your pause or close a job on Indeed, changes will be synced automatically to your page via the plugin.
* Increase reach to possible candidates via visibility on Indeed and your WordPress page
    - Via posting your jobs on Indeed too, more possible candidates will see your job listing.
* Offer a mobile optimized application form via Indeed Apply.

**Where it gets even better:**

Once you created your Indeed Employer Account, you will not only get the increased reach from posting your jobs on Indeed too, but you can use the following services:

* Review applications
* Manage candidates
* Schedule interviews

**How will this plugin help you find the right candidate for your company?**

If you are already using Indeed to post jobs, you can simply generate a list of all your live jobs on your WordPress page.

Just follow the simple steps:

1. Simply login with your Indeed account credentials via the plugin.
2. Decide where to show your jobs list, simply copy and paste the shortcode to a new page or into an existing space. It is up to you!

== Installation ==

1. Upload the 'indeed-jobs' directory to the '/wp-content/plugins/' directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Visit the Indeed Jobs Settings Page under the 'Settings' Menu in the WordPress Admin

= Basic Usage =

The Indeed Jobs Plugin shortcode can be added to any post/page/custom post type. Use the code anywhere you would normally your job listings.

The basic Indeed Jobs Plugin shortcode:

[indeed-jobs]

You only need to copy the shortcode once and a list of all your live jobs on Indeed will be generated.



== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What is the Indeed Jobs plugin? =

This plugin allows you to showcase all your active Indeed job postings and add them to your WordPress page. This way you can reach even more possible candidates.

These jobs are automatically synced by your Indeed dashboard. There is no updating your jobs anywhere else than on Indeed once you implemented the plugin.

= Does this cost anything? =

There is no cost associated with setting up a the Indeed Jobs plugin.

= How do I set up this plugin? =

Just follow the installation steps

= How will a Jobs benefit me? =

Your jobs will not only be visible on your own branded WordPress page. But on Indeed, the world's #1 job site too.

= Are my Jobs jobs visible on Indeed or vice-versa? =

Yes, the job feed is identical on your Jobs plugin and on Indeed.

= Do I have to update my Jobs each time I upload a new job? =

No, Indeed will automatically update the list of jobs, every time you publish a new job in Indeed.

= My potential candidates search for jobs on mobile phones. Will this page work for them? =

Yes, all Jobs made via Indeed are optimized for desktop and mobile.

= What are the benefits of using this service? =

This will increase visibility to your postings, and attract passive candidates to your open requisitions.
